About MBTI

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) assesses personality across four dimensions:

  • E/I (Extraversion/Introversion): Where you get your energy from
  • S/N (Sensing/Intuition): How you process information
  • T/F (Thinking/Feeling): How you make decisions
  • J/P (Judging/Perceiving): How you approach the outside world

These four dimensions combine to form 16 distinct personality types, each with unique characteristics and preferences.
